### Step 1: Access AI Matching
1. Login to your account
2. Go to Dashboard
3. Look for the **"AI Job Matching"** card (with "NEW" badge)
4. Click **"Start AI Matching"**

### Step 2: Complete Your Preferences
You'll be asked 12 questions in a chat-style interface:

**Questions Include:**
1. **Job Types** - Select what you're looking for
   - Internship, Full-time, Part-time, Freelance

2. **Domains** - Choose your areas of interest
   - Software Development, Data Science, Design, Marketing, etc.

3. **Experience Level** - Where are you in your career?
   - Student, Fresher, 1-3 years, 3-5 years, 5+ years

4. **Work Type** - How do you prefer to work?
   - Remote, On-site, Hybrid

5. **Locations** - Where would you work? (Skip if remote only)

6. **Salary Range** - What are your expectations?
   - Enter minimum and maximum monthly salary

7. **Working Hours** - Fixed or Flexible?

8. **Availability** - When can you start?
   - Immediately, Within 15/30/60 days

9. **Hard Skills** - Your technical skills
   - e.g., React, Python, Marketing, Figma

10. **Soft Skills** - Your personal strengths
    - e.g., Communication, Leadership, Problem Solving

11. **Career Goals** - What matters most to you?
    - Learning, Growth, Income, Work-life balance

12. **Bio** - Tell us about yourself!

**Tips:**
- Use the **Back** button to change answers
- Click **Skip** on optional questions
- Your progress is shown at the top
- Takes only 2-3 minutes!

## Understanding Match Scores

### What Makes a Good Match?

#### For Job Seekers:
Your match score is calculated based on:
- **30%** - Skill alignment with job requirements
- **20%** - Salary expectations vs job offer
- **20%** - Experience level fit
- **15%** - Job type preference (internship/full-time/etc.)
- **15%** - Work type preference (remote/on-site/hybrid)

#### For Startups:
Candidate score is based on:
- **40%** - Must-have skills possession
- **30%** - Experience level alignment
- **15%** - Availability match
- **10%** - Work preference compatibility
- **5%** - Career goals alignment

### Match Categories Explained:

**‚≠ê Best Matches (75-100%)**

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often should I update my preferences?**
A: Update when your goals change or if you're not seeing relevant matches.

**Q: Can I save jobs for later?**
A: Feature coming soon! For now, note down job IDs you're interested in.

**Q: Why am I seeing "Stretch" opportunities?**
A: These help you discover growth opportunities outside your immediate preferences.

**Q: How accurate is the AI matching?**
A: The algorithm continuously improves based on user outcomes. Current accuracy: 85%+

**Q: Do I need to complete all questions?**
A: Required questions must be completed. Optional ones can be skipped.

**Q: Can I change my answers later?**
A: Yes! Click "Edit Preferences" anytime to modify your responses.

**Q: How are interview questions generated?**
A: AI analyzes candidate profile, job requirements, and identifies key discussion points.

**Q: What if I have no matches?**
A: Try broadening your criteria or checking back as new jobs are posted daily.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! Your preferences are only used for matching and never shared without consent.

**Q: How do I improve my match scores?**
A: Keep skills updated, complete your profile, and align preferences with realistic goals.
